About Me

Born and raised in New York City, I've always had a love for writing. It was in high school where I strengthened my writing and creativity. As a result, I've written three of my 4 current books while still in highschool. I tried publishing in the past. Unfortunately the editing service provided was less than what I expected. This led me to learn how to evaluate, and edit, a manuscript on my own. As the author, I am my own worst critic and was certain to make my books as ready for publication as I possibly could. Now, the first book is weeks away from being released for publication with more to follow.
